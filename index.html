Roadmap — BSO (Total Supply: 100,000,000)

The plan for a resilient, people-first network. Timelines adapt as we prioritize impact and security.

Q3 2025 — Network Launch

Mainnet validators, secure RPC (rpc.bitsoley.com), explorer, wallet app, chain parameters finalized.

Q4 2025 — Developer Onboarding

One-click add network, faucet for builders, SDKs, grants for education & health pilots, community governance groundwork.

Q1 2026 — Security & Staking

Audits, validator expansion, light client, state sync & snapshot tooling.

Q2 2026 — Real-World Integrations

On-chain credentials, clinic funding rails, agricultural traceability, NGO partnerships, mobile-first UX.
